Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MICS

What are the school hours?
School starts at 8:00 AM and ends at 3:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
How do I apply for admission?
You can apply online via our application portal or download the form from our website.
Is there a school uniform?
Yes, all students are required to wear the official school uniform. See the Uniforms page for details.
How can parents get involved?
Parents are encouraged to join the PTA and participate in school events and meetings.
