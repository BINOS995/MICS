About MICS

AKWAABA!

At Mirekua International Community School (MICS), we're passionate about preparing our students for the future.

As a STEM school, we focus on developing technical skills, fostering problem-solving abilities, promoting innovation and creativity, and providing hands-on learning experiences.

Our interdisciplinary education approach ensures that our students are proficient with current technologies and are adaptable to emerging technological advancements, while also exposing them to various career paths in STEM fields.

Student Life

Campus life for students often involves a mix of academics, social activities, hands-on practical skills and personal growth. It includes attending classes, participating in clubs and sports activities using campus facilities, and forming connections with fellow students.

Enrollment

To enroll in Mirekua International School, you must follow the admission process outlined by MICS. This process usually involves filling out an admission form, providing necessary documents such as previous school reports, and paying any required fees. Additionally, you will sit an entrance exam.
