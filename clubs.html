CLUBS

Welcome to the World of Clubs at Mirekua International Community School!
Our clubs ignite student passion, cultivate essential skills, and go beyond the classroom. Students engage in diverse activities, discover their talents, and nurture teamwork. We offer a vibrant range of clubs that encourage holistic growth through dynamic offerings.

Powerboys

Empowering the Boy Child: The Powerboys Club is dedicated to nurturing confident, responsible, and well-rounded young men. Through engaging workshops, mentorship, and practical life lessons, boys learn essential skills, values, and etiquette that prepare them for leadership and positive impact in their communities.

Girls Corner

Celebrating Girlhood: Girls Corner is a vibrant space where girls discover their strengths, creativity, and voice. Members participate in hands-on activities, health and wellness sessions, and inspirational talks, all designed to foster self-esteem, independence, and lifelong friendships.
