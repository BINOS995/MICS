ABOUT MIREKUA ICS

Established in 2021, Mirekua International Community School (MICS) is fundamentally a Christian school that seeks to instill Godly values into children, as we also shape up their destinies to be responsible members of society.

MICS provides exceptional academic education to children within a friendly environment, by our highly experienced and passionate staff. We hire the best staff to help train our students and bring out their God-given talents, skills and abilities.

What distinguishes our school from all the others is that we are focused on ensuring our graduates become well-rounded citizens of their community and country at large.

We are able to accomplish this by first ensuring they excel in their academics, and subsequently identifying potential areas that they either exhibit interest in, or we can harness, and develop those areas accordingly.

By so doing, our graduates do not only become "book smart", but also develop diverse soft skills and technical skills which can be used in life.

With your child in our hands, you can be rest assured that we will develop them to be the "Cornerstone of Excellence" for their generation.

Our Mission & Vision

VISION

Using Godly principles to develop children to be the "Cornerstone of Excellence" in their generation.

MISSION

VALUES
