How to Use SmartApp for Learners and Parents

Welcome to SmartApp! Empowering parents and learners with easy access to school updates, homework, attendance, and direct communicationâ€”all in one place.

For Parents
  • Step 1: Download SmartApp from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
  • Step 2: Register as a Parent using your phone number and student details.
  • Step 3: Explore features: Attendance tracking, Homework updates, and Direct Communication with teachers and school.
For Learners
  • Step 1: Download SmartApp from your device's app store.
  • Step 2: Register as a Student using your school ID or registration code.
  • Step 3: Use SmartApp to join classes, check homework, and message your teachers.
Quick Tips
Keep your login details safe
Enable notifications for instant updates
Contact support via the app for help
Update the app regularly for new features
