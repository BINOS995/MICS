ACADEMICS AT MICS

Home Academics

MICS is committed to delivering quality education that builds future-ready leaders through the Ghana Education Service curriculum.

At MICS, we are dedicated to providing a comprehensive education that covers the Ghana Education Service Primary School curriculum from preschool to JHS. We use the latest teaching techniques and innovative technology to ensure that each student is challenged and engaged in their education. Our experienced teachers use creative teaching techniques that inspire a lifelong love for learning in our students. With a strong emphasis on academics, we’ll help your child develop the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in the future. Our teachers are experienced professionals who use innovative approaches to make learning engaging, interactive, and fun. We believe in providing a positive and nurturing environment where students can thrive and reach their full potential.

At MICS, we are dedicated to shaping the future leaders of tomorrow through a comprehensive education journey that spans from preschool to Junior High School, following the Ghana Education Service Primary School curriculum.

Preschool (Ages 6 months – 6 years)

Subjects:

  • Literacy
  • Numeracy
  • Geography

In our preschool classes, we focus on early childhood development through hands-on learning, play-based activities, and caring guidance. Our goal is to build strong foundational skills in:

  • Literacy: Early reading, phonics, storytelling, vocabulary building.
  • Numeracy: Counting, sorting, number recognition, patterns, and basic problem-solving.

Why MICS Preschool?

Our nurturing environment, combined with engaging lessons and creative exploration, helps children grow cognitively, emotionally, and socially while building the confidence to take their first steps in education.

Primary School (Grades 1 – 6 | Ages 6 – 11)

Subjects:

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Computing
  • History
  • Creative Arts
  • French
  • Twi

Our Primary School curriculum is designed to inspire curiosity, encourage exploration, and foster independence in learning. We provide a balanced academic experience that strengthens core skills while developing talents and character.

Learning Highlights:

  • English Language & Literacy: Reading fluency, grammar, comprehension, and writing skills.
  • Mathematics: Practical problem-solving, critical thinking, and foundational numeracy.
  • Science: Discovery-based learning that makes students explore the world around them.
  • Computing: Digital literacy and coding basics using modern tools.
  • History & Twi: Cultural awareness, identity, and national heritage.
  • French: Basic vocabulary, conversational skills, and fun language games.
  • Creative Arts: Drawing, painting, drama, music, and creative expression.

Our classrooms are full of energy, imagination, and innovation – where students learn not just to remember, but to understand and apply.

Junior High School (Grades 7 – 9 | Ages 11 – 14)

Subjects:

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Computing
  • Social Studies
  • French
  • RME (Religious & Moral Education)
  • Career Technology
  • Creative Arts & Design
  • Twi

At this stage, we prepare our students for BECE and the transition to Senior High School by deepening academic knowledge and promoting independent learning.

Core Focus Areas:

  • English & Communication: Analytical reading, structured writing, and effective communication.
  • Advanced Mathematics & Science: Applying logic, scientific reasoning, and real-world applications.
  • Computing & Career Tech: Hands-on skills in ICT, programming, and practical life skills.
  • RME & Twi: Moral development, ethics, and cultural identity.
  • French & Creative Arts: Expanding language proficiency and creative expression.

We equip students with skills for the 21st century – innovation, collaboration, digital fluency, and ethical leadership.

Students graduate from MICS with:

  • A solid academic foundation
  • Critical thinking and problem-solving abilities
  • A positive mindset and the confidence to lead

We are shaping the future leaders of tomorrow.
